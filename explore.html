Frequently Asked Questions

❓ Which package is right for my business?
If you're just starting out, Basic packages are perfect. For growing businesses, Standard packages offer the best value. Premium packages are ideal for established businesses needing advanced features.
❓ Can I upgrade my package later?
Yes! You can upgrade from Basic to Standard or Premium at any time. You only pay the difference in price.
❓ What is the payment process?
We require 50% advance payment to start the project, and the remaining 50% upon completion. We accept KBZ Pay, Wave Pay, and bank transfers.
❓ How long does delivery take?
Delivery times vary by package. Basic: 2-7 days, Standard: 7-21 days, Premium: 14-45 days. Exact timeline is provided in your quotation.
❓ Do you offer revisions?
Yes! Basic includes 1 revision, Standard includes 2 revisions, and Premium includes unlimited revisions. Additional revisions beyond the package limit may incur extra charges.
❓ Can I get a custom package?
Absolutely! If our standard packages don't fit your needs, contact us for a custom quotation tailored to your specific requirements.
